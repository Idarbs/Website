About Me

Hello and welcome to my website. I am looking to start a career in IT and this website serves as a portfolio to show off my skills and projects. In the beginning, I had no idea what I wanted to do with myself. My original plan was to become a programmer, I found out early on that this path was not for me. One day someone recommended to me that I try cybersecurity. I had a roadmap and I followed it. I got my CompTIA Security+ and moved onto the Cisco CCNA. It was there when I realized what I wanted to with my life. I wanted to work with networks.

I have experience in networking, Linux, scripting, and more. Please feel free to explore the website and check out my projects on the portfolio page. In my free time I like to read, run, go to the gym, and play games. I am always looking to learn new skills and take on new challenges in the IT field. You can also find more information about me on my LinkedIn profile. Thank you for visiting!
